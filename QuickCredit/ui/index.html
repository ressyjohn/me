How to use

In 3 easy steps, you can conveniently get that loan you really need.

1

Create an account by signing up to our service and providing relevant details about yourself.

2

Create a loan request where you specify the amount of money you will need and the time it will take for you to pay up.

3

Your loan request will be processed and you will get a feedback in less than 5 working days.
